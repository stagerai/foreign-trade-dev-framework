# 第4轮邮件模板：破冰询问

**适用轮次：** 第4轮（沉默3轮后，打破僵局）
**篇幅要求：** 30-40词（最短）
**策略核心：** 直接询问"Should I stop contacting you?"，给客户Yes/No选项
**CTA类型：** Yes or No?

---

## 毅冰铁律（必须遵守）

| 铁律 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| **主题行必须直接** | Should I stop contacting you? |
| **承认多轮沉默** | 说"I've reached out X times without a reply" |
| **给Yes/No选项** | 不需要思考，直接回复 |
| **不给压力** | 说"If not interested, I understand" |
| **与第1-3轮角度不同** | 破冰询问，不是推销 |

---

## 主题行设计

| 合格主题 | 说明 |
|---------|------|
| Should I stop contacting you? | 毅冰经典破冰主题 |
| Quick question: Yes or No? | 简洁版 |
| Should I continue following up? | 完整版 |

---

## 正文结构

```
主题：Should I stop contacting you?

Hi [姓名],

[承认多轮沉默，1句]。[简单价值重申，1句]。[直接询问，1句]。

Yes or No works.

Best regards,
[Your Name] | [YourCo]
```

---

## 合格模板示例

### 示例1：经典破冰（毅冰原版）

```
主题：Should I stop contacting you?

Hi John,

I've reached out 3 times without a reply. If industrial networking isn't a priority for ExampleCo right now, I understand.

Should I stop contacting you? Yes or No works.

Best regards,
[Your Name] | [YourCo]
```

**词数：** 36词
**主题：** Should I stop contacting you?
**承认沉默：** reached out 3 times
**CTA：** Yes or No works

### 示例2：给退路版

```
主题：Should I stop contacting you?

Hi Jane,

I've emailed 3 times about industrial switches for ExampleCo. If this isn't a priority, no problem - I'll stop.

Should I stop? Yes or No.

Best regards,
[Your Name] | [YourCo]
```

**词数：** 35词
**给退路：** If this isn't a priority, no problem

---

## 第4轮回复处理

### 客户回复"Yes"

24小时内回复，简短感谢 + 发送价值信息，不再推销，转为对话模式。

```
主题：Re: Should I stop contacting you?

Hi [姓名],

Thanks for confirming. Quick question: what's most relevant for [公司名] right now?
- Pricing vs current supplier?
- Technical specs for projects?
- Delivery time for urgent orders?

Let me know what to focus on.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### 客户回复"No"

礼貌回复，转长线培养，每季度问候。

```
主题：Re: Should I stop contacting you?

Hi [姓名],

Understood. I'll stop following up.

If [产品/服务] ever becomes relevant, feel free to reach out.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### 客户仍沉默

转长线培养（第5轮+），每季度问候，不推销，不给压力。

## 评分标准（第4轮）

| 维度 | 满分 | 第4轮要求 |
|------|------|---------|
| 主题行具体性 | 20 | **Should I stop contacting you?** |
| 主题行长度 | 10 | ≤10词 |
| 正文篇幅 | 15 | **≤40词**（最短） |
| 差异化 | 20 | **与第1-3轮主题/角度完全不同** |
| 价值具体性 | 15 | 简单重申价值（可选） |
| CTA门槛 | 10 | **Yes or No** |
| 联系人适配 | 10 | 适合联系人角色 |

## 第4轮检查清单

```
□ 主题行为 Should I stop contacting you? 或 Yes or No
□ 主题行与第1-3轮不重复（必须）
□ 正文 ≤40 词（最短）
□ 正文包含：承认多轮沉默 + 给退路
□ CTA为 Yes or No
□ 不给压力
□ 不推销产品（只问是否继续）
```
