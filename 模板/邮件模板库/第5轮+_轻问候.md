# 第5轮+邮件模板：轻问候

**适用轮次：** 第5轮及以后（转长线培养）
**篇幅要求：** 30-50词
**策略核心：** 行业信息/季节性问候，不推销，不给压力
**CTA类型：** Stay in touch（无回复压力）

---

## 毅冰铁律（必须遵守）

| 铁律 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| **不推销** | "转长线培养，每季度问候一次，不给压力" |
| **提供行业价值** | 分享行业动态、原材料价格、市场趋势 |
| **零压力** | "Stay in touch whenever needed" |
| **与第1-4轮角度不同** | 不是推销，是保持联系 |

---

## 主题行设计

| 要素 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| **行业动态** | [Raw material] price update — Q3 locked |
| **季节性** | Hope Q3 is off to a good start |
| **展会/活动** | [Trade show name] — any plans to attend? |
| **技术趋势** | [Industry trend] — thought you might find this useful |

---

## 正文结构

```
主题：[行业动态/原材料/季节性话题]

Hi [姓名],

[行业信息，不推销]。[简单问候，不给压力]。

Stay in touch whenever needed.

Best regards,
[Your Name] | [YourCo]
```

---

## 合格模板示例

### 示例1：原材料价格

```
主题：[Material] price update — Q3 locked

Hi John,

[Material] prices surged 30% in Q1. Many brands raised prices 15-20%. We locked contracts early — stable pricing through Q3 for our contacts.

Stay in touch whenever needed.

Best regards,
[Your Name] | [YourCo]
```

### 示例2：季节性问候

```
主题：Hope Q3 is off to a good start

Hi Jane,

Just a quick note to say hello. Hope Q3 is off to a good start for ExampleCo. We're still here if industrial networking ever comes up.

No need to reply — just staying in touch.

Best regards,
[Your Name] | [YourCo]
```

### 示例3：行业趋势

```
主题：[Industry trend] — thought you might find this interesting

Hi Mike,

Saw this [industry article/report] and thought of ExampleCo's work. If you haven't seen it: [brief 1-line summary].

No agenda — just thought you'd find it useful.

Best regards,
[Your Name] | [YourCo]
```

## 评分标准（第5轮+）

| 维度 | 满分 | 第5轮+要求 |
|------|------|----------|
| 主题行具体性 | 20 | 行业动态/季节性话题 |
| 主题行长度 | 10 | ≤10词 |
| 正文篇幅 | 15 | **≤50词** |
| 差异化 | 20 | **与第1-4轮角度完全不同** |
| 价值具体性 | 15 | 含行业信息/数据 |
| CTA门槛 | 10 | Stay in touch / No need to reply |
| 联系人适配 | 10 | 适合联系人角色 |

---

## 第5轮+检查清单

```
□ 主题行为行业动态/季节性话题（不推销）
□ 主题行与第1-4轮不重复（必须）
□ 正文 ≤50 词
□ 正文不推销产品
□ 不给压力
□ CTA为 Stay in touch 或 No need to reply
□ 每季度问候一次
```
